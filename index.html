My Capstone Project Outline

Posted on 2018-01-23

My project is about making math study guides (both calculus and precalculus). More details are given below.

Goals

General Goal

Write math study guides for grade 10 and 11 students. (Calculus and Precalculus stuff)

Specific Goal

The two study guides should cover all the chapters and contents that will be tested in SAT2 Math/AP Calculus BC.

Advertise the guides to grade 10 and 11 students and at least 30 students use them.

The Average feedback score of the study guides needs to be at least 9/10.

Project Benchmarks Outline

The followings are my plan about my timeline.

Jan.24th - Finish building my capstone website (which is this one) and a study guide website. Click here to view the study guide one: Server

Feb.2nd - Finish summarizing all the stuffs that need to be written.

Feb.20th - Finish writing the two study guides in pdf form. During the process of writing the guides, update the content to the study guide website as well.

Mar.20th - Advertise the guides to G10 and G11 students and there needs to be at least 30 people using the study guides.

Mar.27th - Design and receive feedbacks from all of the users and make a formal written statistics analysis.
