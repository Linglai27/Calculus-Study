Reflection

Posted on 2018-04-07

I want to do this project as I want to help those having trouble learning mathematics. My goal is to write a calculus tutorial and then make someone uses it. During the execution process, I actually found it difficult to start. I had no idea what a common study guide looked like. So I referenced AP Barron Calculus and another college-level calculus book. Then I spent a a little bit time structuring my tutorial and started writing the tutorial. Now I have already finished writing the tutorial but have not made anybody use it. The major thing I learned through this project is that you have to always remember the core of your tasks. I was distracted by some calculus textbooks and was amazed that their covers and images were cool. But I did not realize that actually I had only at most 50 hours. Editing the contents were much more important than design of the study guide.

Reference:

  1. The Calculus Textbook I used
  2. Paul’s online math notes
  3. AP Calculus BC Barron
